GenericCharacterTables.normalize — Method
normalize(a::FracPoly)

Return a normal form b of a such that $\exp(2\pi \mathrm{i} \cdot a) = \exp(2\pi \mathrm{i} \cdot b)$.

This is done by reducing the numerators of the coefficients modulo their denominators.

GenericCharacterTables.shift_char_parameters — Method
shift_char_parameters(t::CharTable{T}, a::Union{Parameters{T},Cyclotomic{T}}, steps::Int64) where T <: NfPoly

Replace all character parameters of t in a by their counterparts suffixed with steps.

This is done by shifting them steps*nrparams(t) steps further in t.argumentring.

GenericCharacterTables.simplify — Method
simplify(x::Union{Cyclotomic{T}, ParameterException{T}}, c::T, cinv::Generic.FracFieldElem{T}) where T <: NfPoly

Simplify x by replacing the generator of the polynomials of type T by c and then back to cinv.

This is used to remove terms that look fractional but are in fact whole.
